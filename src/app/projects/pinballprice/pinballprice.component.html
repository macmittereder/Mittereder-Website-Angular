Pinball Price

This app was a contractor project through Blast Off Apps developed for Android with Android Studio and iOS with Xcode.Pinballprice.com is the worlds largest selection of pinball machines to either buy or find more information on a specific one. The app displays their website using a slide out menu for navigation and a webview. Their webpages were also modified for the app to remove the navigation bar and padding from the side upon clients request. However, the subdomain for this project has been removed by the the owner of Pinballprice.com

The slide out side menu made navigating through the website easier for users.
